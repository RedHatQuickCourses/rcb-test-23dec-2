Hands-on Lab

This section provides a practical, step-by-step guide to installing and configuring the components of RHEL Lightspeed. Participants will gain hands-on experience in deploying and managing RHEL Lightspeed, a tool designed to accelerate development by automating routine tasks, enhancing security, and streamlining compliance. The lab will cover:

  • Downloading and preparing the necessary installation media

  • Installing RHEL Lightspeed on a Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) system

  • Configuring RHEL Lightspeed policies to meet specific development needs

  • Verifying the successful installation and configuration of RHEL Lightspeed components

By the end of this lab, learners will have a solid understanding of how to implement RHEL Lightspeed in their own environments, thereby accelerating their development processes.
